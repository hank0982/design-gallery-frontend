In the first phase of the study, you will (a) Learn six principles of visual design (10 minutes) (b) Take a survey about your knowledge in design principles (5 minutes) Now, please click ‘Next’ to start phase 1 of the study.

In the second phase of the study, you will

(a) Create a poster based on a design prompt (45 minutes)

(b) Complete a survey about your experience creating the poster (10 minutes)

Now, please click ‘Next’ to start phase 2 of the study. We hope you enjoy the task, and we look forward to seeing your creativ e solution!

In the third phase of the study, you will use a popular online design platform- Behance to browse and search for example posters. Behance is a design platform where designers showcase their projects and solicit encouragement or suggestions from other members. Your goal is to find two examples that are useful and inspirational for improving your own poster.

In this phase of the study, you will:
(a) Review your reflection on the poster. (5 minutes)
(b) Browse the gallery and identify helpful example designs. (25 minutes)

Now, please click ‘Next’ to start phase 3 of the study.

In the final phase of the study, you will complete the following steps:

(a) Revise your poster to better follow the design principles. (20 minutes)
(b) Complete a survey about your design process. (5 minutes)

Please have a copy of your initial design ready in the design tool or software of your choice.
