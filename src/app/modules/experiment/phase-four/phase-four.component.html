Phase 4 - Revise your poster based on your learnings about design principles (20 minutes)

Please invest around 20 minutes to revise your design based on what you learned from the reflection process and the gallery examples. As mentioned previously, the revised designs will be rated by design experts, and the five designs rated the highest will be awarded a $40 prize. Similar to the previous poster creation phase, we will also be asking you to keep a stopwatch running to measure how much time has passed for this phase.

Before you begin your revisions, please review your reflection on your design process from Phase 3. Once you have finished reviewing your reflection and have reminded yourself of the requirements of the design, please start your stopwatch and begin revising.

Once you have revised your design, please submit an image of it to this page. Again, be aware that once you submit your design, no further changes can be made to your design. We hope you enjoy this design task, and we look forward to your submission!
