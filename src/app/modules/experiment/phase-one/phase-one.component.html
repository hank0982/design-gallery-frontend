Phase 1a - Learn Design Principles (10 minutes)

Before creating your own poster, please take approximately 10 minutes to review the six principles of visual design. These principles are rules that should be followed in order to create an effective and attractive composition of a poster, banner, advertisement, infographic, and other forms of visual media.

After reviewing the principles, please click the green button at the end of the page to take a short survey about the principles.

In the next phase, you will need to create a poster that follows one or more of these principles. You will have access to this page throughout the study session.
